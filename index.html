FootBall

The HSF ACADEMY provides aspiring footballers with professional class training to reach the next level. At The School campus, we offer a Professional Football Coaching Program with experienced coaches licenced by AIFF, where the monitoring and evaluation of the student's performance will be done by the professionals.

Competitive and friendly match opportunities on a regular basis will be arranged with quality opponents and talented students will get an opportunity to play at the BFC Youth Academy teams. Students will also be provided Sports Science expertise (Strength & Conditioning, Nutrition and Injury Prevention concepts).
